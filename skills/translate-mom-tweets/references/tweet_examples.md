# Tweet Template Examples

These examples demonstrate the hook-driven, curiosity-gap tweet style for translated video content.

## Structure

Every tweet follows this pattern:
1. **Hook** (first line) — dramatic, curiosity-inducing one-liner drawn from the video's content
2. **Context** (second line) — brief setup explaining the situation
3. **Translation CTA** (last line) — emphasizes the video is translated to the target language

## Examples

### Confrontation / Argument style
```
This man told his wife she can't cook... in front of her entire family 😳

A heated family dinner goes completely off the rails when one comment changes everything.

Fully translated to English 🎬👇
[video link]
```

### Shocking revelation style
```
She found out her husband's secret after 10 years... and recorded everything 🤯

What started as a normal phone check turned into the most shocking discovery.

Now translated to English so you can follow every word 🔥
[video link]
```

### Challenge / Competition style
```
This street vendor challenged a Michelin chef... and won 🏆

Nobody expected this roadside cook to absolutely destroy a professional chef in a blind taste test.

Watch the full showdown — translated to English 🍳
[video link]
```

### Emotional / Wholesome style
```
A father sees his daughter for the first time in 15 years... 😢

After being separated by war, this reunion will break you.

Translated to English — every word hits different 💔
[video link]
```

### Mystery / Suspense style
```
Nobody believed this fisherman's story... until he showed the footage 📹

He said he caught something impossible. Everyone laughed. Then he pulled out his phone.

Full story translated to English 🌊
[video link]
```

## Key principles

- **Lead with the most dramatic moment** from the SRT content — don't build up to it
- **Use real names when available** in the subtitles for authenticity; fall back to "this man", "this woman", "she", "he" when names aren't mentioned
- **One emoji per line max** — don't over-decorate
- **Keep it under 280 characters total** (Twitter/X limit)
- **Always mention the target language** in the CTA line
- **Include the video link** placeholder at the end
- **The hook must make someone stop scrolling** — if you'd scroll past it, rewrite it
